IMPORTANT INFORMATION
Thank you for carefully reading the following:
Modes of Settlement of Operational Expenses
1.	One payment: full settlement before 31 January 2013 carries 5% discount.
2.	Two payments: half before 15 February and half before end April 2013.
3.	Six interest-bearing monthly installments starting before 15 February 2013. 
(Thank you for checking with Membership Accounts for credit terms). 
Penalty: non-commitment to one of the modes of payment, above, will result in the following 
charge to your 2013 account: 5% on 1 March 2013 and 5% on 1 May 2013. 
Membership Cards 
  Cards are delivered upon the settlement of dues and presentation of new passport size photos.
  Members are kindly reminded that the presentation of valid Membership Cards is essential 
  for Club access. 
Complimentary Invitations
20 free invitation vouchers will be delivered along with the Membership Cards. These are valid from 
1 January 2013 to 31 January 2014, except for the days specified on the Invitation booklets. Invitations may not be exchanged or transferred. Management will not replace lost or obsolete invitation vouchers.  
Non-Residents 
Members living abroad for 9 months or more per year may apply for the non-resident status discount. 
Changes in Family Status
Thank you for communicating changes in family status before 15 December 2012. Fourth Child
Families will only be charged for the 3 eldest children. 
Vignette
 Please make sure your 2013 vignette is clearly displayed on the left hand side of the windscreen.  
 The Club will only admit cars with valid vignettes into the covered car park.
Guests
Guests can only access the Club in the company of the inviting Member who must be present during the whole stay of the Guest at the Club. The same Guest may not access the Club over twice a week.
